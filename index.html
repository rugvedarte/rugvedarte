About Me

Having co-founded a startup, I am proficient at using analytical skills and a data-oriented approach for making informed business decisions. It was then that I realized the importance of creating a good user experience through adequate user research and iterative development. I believe that usability and experience are just as crucial for a product, as its technology. This inspiration led me towards pursuing Information Science at the University of Michigan, School of Information.

Creativity and analytical thinking have always been my greatest strengths. Putting them to good use, I wish to design products that are user-centered along with being innovative in their approach.
